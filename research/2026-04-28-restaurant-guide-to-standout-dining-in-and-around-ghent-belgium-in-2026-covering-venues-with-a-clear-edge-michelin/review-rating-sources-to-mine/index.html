Decision. Anchor the guide on two critic guides — Michelin[1] and Gault&Millau Belux 2026 (1,340 addresses, scored 0–20 + toques)[2][3]. Triangulate volume + freshness with TheFork (20M verified reviews, booking-gated)[4] and Resto.be (250k reviews / 17.5k Belgian restaurants — the local default)[5]. Use TripAdvisor only as a tourist-bias counterweight[6]; reach for Spotted by Locals[7] and the VRT NWS map[8] for local press signal.

Sources, ranked

Source Signal Coverage Best for Watch out
Michelin Guide[1] Stars, Bib Gourmand, Plate; inspector text Curated; missing many strong Ghent rooms Anchor for "clear edge" — automatic shortlist Conservative; biased toward fine-dining grammar
Gault&Millau BE[9] 0–20 score + 1–5 toques (15/20 = 2 toques)[3] 1,340 BE/LUX addresses, 154 newcomers in 2026[2] Granular ranking + H!P trend list (Martino won Flanders 2026)[2] Paywall; print guide €34
Resto.be[5] Belgian-native ratings, ~250k reviews 17,500 BE restaurants — densest local set Volume + recent-review recency for shortlist QA Some operator-curated content; verify outliers
TheFork[4] Verified post-booking reviews (20M total) Strong on mid/upper Ghent; weaker on hole-in-wall Filter fakes; book-only review = high trust → score Promo discounts skew which restaurants list
TripAdvisor[6] Tourist volume, ranked lists Nearly every Ghent venue Reality-check: places that score on critic guides AND TA top 50 → ⚠ tourist-trap risk Heavy tourist bias; downweight
Google Maps reviews Highest-volume crowd rating Universal; recency strong Cross-check star drift over last 12 months No API for free scrape at scale; ToS
Spotted by Locals[7] Locally-written editorial picks ~19 Ghent venues Counter-tourist signal; small but hand-picked Tiny N; use as positive vote, not negative
VRT NWS map[8] Public-broadcaster recap of G&M winners Belgium-wide Free, structured, NL-language; quick map import Derived from G&M; don't double-count
Reddit r/belgium, r/Ghent; TripAdvisor Ghent forum Qualitative threads, locals vs visitors Patchy Hype reality-check; surface niche picks Low N per venue; treat as anecdote

Mining strategy

  • Build the shortlist from Michelin ∪ Gault&Millau ≥14/20 — these are the "clear edge" filters by definition[3].
  • Rank inside the shortlist with Resto.be + TheFork volume × rating; require a verified-review floor (e.g. ≥50 reviews) to drop one-hit wonders[5][4].
  • Flag tourist-traps: high TripAdvisor rank + low Resto.be/TheFork engagement → ⚠ skip[6].
  • Enrich blurbs from Spotted by Locals + Reddit threads for the local angle[7].
