## The flagship picks

### NDC family — Oslo, Copenhagen, AI, TechTown, (Porto TBA)

NDC Conferences is the centre of gravity for European .NET content. The 2026 calendar runs Sydney → Toronto → Copenhagen → AI → Oslo → TechTown → London 2027 [[1]](https://ndcconferences.com/). For senior devs, **NDC Oslo** is still the headline: 5 days, 150 speakers being booked, names like Troy Hunt, Kevlin Henney, Venkat Subramaniam, Nick Chapsas [[4]](https://ndcoslo.com/). All-Access Early Bird is 22,490 NOK (~€1,940) ending 22 May; Regular is 25,990 NOK (~€2,240) [[3]](https://ndcoslo.com/tickets) — both before 25% Norwegian VAT.

**NDC Copenhagen** is the cheaper, shorter alternative with similar speaker DNA: 4 days at Øksnehallen [[5]](https://ndccopenhagen.com/), All-Access at 15,900 DKK (~€2,130) +VAT or conference-only at 9,900 DKK (~€1,330) [[2]](https://ndccopenhagen.com/tickets).

**NDC AI** (8-10 June, Oslo) [[7]](https://ndc-ai.com/) and **NDC TechTown** (21-24 September, embedded / systems) [[9]](https://ndctechtown.com/) are narrower spin-offs; pick AI if you've already done a generalist NDC and want depth, TechTown if you write firmware/IoT C#.

**NDC Porto 2026** has not announced dates as of May 2026 — historical pattern is mid-to-late October [[6]](https://ndcporto.com/). Watch the site if Portugal is your preferred destination.

**NDC London** for 2026 already happened (late January) [[10]](https://ndclondon.com/); next QEII Centre slot is 25-29 January 2027.

### Techorama Belgium — the home pick

If you're based in Belgium, this is the no-brainer event. Antwerp (Kinepolis cinema venue), 11-13 May 2026, ~150 sessions across .NET / Azure / AI / DevOps / web / data [[11]](https://techorama.be/). Tickets are tiered: €699 Workshop-only (May 11), €899 Knight (conference 12-13, includes all session recordings + merchandise voucher), €1,399 Combi (everything) [[12]](https://techorama.be/tickets). Late-bird price increase kicked in 6 April. The same crew runs **Techorama Netherlands** in Utrecht 26-28 October [[13]](https://www.techorama.nl/) — useful as a fallback if the BE tickets sold out.

The 2026 theme is "Medieval Edition", which is just a vibe, not a content pivot.

### Update Conference Prague — best session-density-per-euro

Two days at the Clarion Congress Hotel, 12-13 November 2026 [[14]](https://prague.updateconference.net/). Four parallel tracks, 40+ technical sessions, ~65 speakers covering .NET / Cloud / Security / AI / Blazor [[14]](https://prague.updateconference.net/). Reputation in CEE is that this is where senior .NET devs from Czechia / Slovakia / Hungary go when they don't fly to Oslo. Live-stream tickets exist if you can't travel.

## The adjacent set — not C#-first, but worth your travel budget

- **DDD Europe** [[21]](https://2026.dddeurope.com/) — language-agnostic by design, but the audience is heavily .NET architects; held in Antwerp 8-12 June, overlapping geographically with Techorama. Two tracks: Foundations (DDD canon) and EventCentric (event-driven).
- **Devoxx Belgium** [[22]](https://devoxx.be/) — Java-anchored but a magnet for cross-stack senior devs; €695 conference ticket is genuinely cheap for the size.
- **Voxxed Days Luxembourg** [[23]](https://luxembourg.voxxeddays.com/en/) — Devoxx-family, Java-led, **no dedicated .NET track** [[23]](https://luxembourg.voxxeddays.com/en/) — skip unless you specifically want to broaden.
- **Voxxed Days Amsterdam** [[24]](https://amsterdam.voxxeddays.com/) — Already past (1-2 April 2026).

## Online / virtual

**.NET Conf** is Microsoft's free virtual annual event in November — the 2025 edition ran 11-13 November, 15th iteration, 100k+ live viewers, .NET 10 + Visual Studio 2026 launch [[25]](https://www.dotnetconf.net/). 2026's date hasn't been announced but the second week of November is the multi-year cadence. Watch parties pop up locally; check local user groups for organised viewings.
