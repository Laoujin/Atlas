Among Belgium/France/Netherlands/Germany venues, the only academic AI/ML conference still accepting submissions is **BNAIC 2026 in Maastricht** (subs open June 1, deadline Aug 31) [[2]](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl/bnaic2026/call_papers). Closest open-CFP NLP venue to the region is **EMNLP 2026 / Budapest** (May 25 via ARR) [[9]](https://2026.emnlp.org/calls/main_conference_papers/) [[10]](https://2026.emnlp.org/); closest CV venue is **BMVC 2026 / Lancaster, UK** (May 29) [[8]](https://bmvc2026.bmva.org/calls/call-for-papers/). The big regional series — ECAI/IJCAI Bremen, ECML PKDD Naples, HHAI Brussels, ICCBR Bremen, UAI Amsterdam, ESANN Bruges — have all already closed.

## If your geography is Belgium / France / Netherlands / Germany

Open CFPs in those four countries are thin — most regional series ran their submission cycle in Q1 2026.

- **BNAIC 2026 / BeNeLearn — Maastricht, NL** (Oct 21–23) — the only academic AI/ML CFP in the four target countries currently accepting work [[2]](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl/bnaic2026/call_papers). Submissions span Type A (long/short original work, 10–15 pp), Type B (encore abstracts of already-published work), Type C (video/demo abstracts), Type D — the **encore track** is the lowest-effort entry if you have a recent paper from elsewhere [[2]](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl/bnaic2026/call_papers).
- **NeurIPS 2026 — Paris node** (Dec 6–12) — Palais des Congrès de Paris is one of three host venues alongside Atlanta and Sydney [[3]](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2026/Dates). Same paper deadline as the main track (May 6) [[1]](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2026/CallForPapers) — pick whichever city you'll attend.
- **EMNLP 2026 — Budapest, HU** (Oct 24–29) — closest open-CFP NLP venue to the four-country region; ARR May 25 [[9]](https://2026.emnlp.org/calls/main_conference_papers/) [[10]](https://2026.emnlp.org/).
- **BMVC 2026 — Lancaster, UK** (Nov 23–26) — closest open-CFP CV venue; abstract May 22, paper May 29 [[8]](https://bmvc2026.bmva.org/calls/call-for-papers/).
