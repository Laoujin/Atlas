## Three caveats on the table

- [**Devoxx France**](https://www.devoxx.fr/en/) **already happened** (22-24 Apr) [[9]](https://www.devoxx.fr/en/2025/11/21/call-for-paper-2026/). For French-speaking architecture content, the next live CFP is [**MiXiT 2026**](https://mixitconf.org/2026/about) [[23]](https://mixitconf.org/2026/about) (Lyon, 16-17 Apr 2026 — also past) and [**BDX I/O**](https://bdxio.fr/) [[24]](https://bdxio.fr/) (Bordeaux, 29-30 Oct 2026; CFP not yet posted). France's 2026 architecture-conference calendar is thin after Devoxx; most of the wave is in 2027.
- [**iSAQB SAG**](https://www.software-architecture-gathering.com/)**'s CFP is only a few days from opening**, not actually open at the moment you read this on 30 Apr [[2]](https://www.software-architecture-gathering.com/call-for-papers/). It's grouped as ⚠ rather than ✓ for that reason — but if architecture is your topic, this is the highest-signal CFP in DE for the year.
- [**Devoxx Belgium**](https://devoxx.be/)**'s CFP is six weeks from opening** [[10]](https://devoxx.be/). Same caveat — flagged for planning, not for submitting today.

## ICSA 2026 — what's still useful even though the main CFP is closed

The 23rd IEEE International Conference on Software Architecture is hosted at VU Amsterdam, 22-26 Jun 2026 [[3]](https://conf.researchr.org/home/icsa-2026). For practitioners, the **Software Architecture in Practice** track (papers due 8 Dec 2025) is closed, but the conference is the densest place this year to encounter recent peer-reviewed architecture research in Europe — workshops, tutorials, and the showcase track all keep their programs visible until the event [[4]](https://conf.researchr.org/dates/icsa-2026). If you want the academic side of the field, attend; if you wanted to publish, that train left in December.

## ECSA 2026 — the European academic counterpart, with multiple still-open windows

[ECSA 2026](https://conf.researchr.org/home/ecsa-2026) (20th European Conference on SW Architecture) is in **Bolzano, Italy, 7-11 Sep 2026** [[5]](https://conf.researchr.org/home/ecsa-2026). Outside the BE/FR/NL/DE scope, but it carries the most active academic-track CFP window in Europe right now:

- Industrial / Tools & Demos / Doctoral Symposium **abstracts due 1 May 2026** (so: tomorrow) [[6]](https://conf.researchr.org/dates/ecsa-2026).
- Industrial papers, workshops, registered reports, tools-demos papers, IDEA-ARCH/ASISAS/CASA abstracts **due 8 May 2026** [[6]](https://conf.researchr.org/dates/ecsa-2026).
- Workshop & co-located papers **due 15 May 2026** [[6]](https://conf.researchr.org/dates/ecsa-2026).
- Open Science track **due 19 Jun**, Journal First **due 26 Jun 2026** [[6]](https://conf.researchr.org/dates/ecsa-2026).

If your goal is "publish architecture work in 2026 with reasonable European reach", ECSA's industrial track at 8 May is the realistic window.

## How to pick

- **Want to submit a talk this week and have it land in 2026?** → [**ECSA**](https://conf.researchr.org/home/ecsa-2026) **industrial abstracts** (May 1) [[6]](https://conf.researchr.org/dates/ecsa-2026) or [**Code BEAM Europe**](https://codebeameurope.com/) (May 31) [[14]](https://codebeameurope.com/) if you work in BEAM, or [**Techorama NL**](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) (May 29) [[17]](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) for general practitioner content. All three pay travel for accepted speakers (ECSA at speaker discretion; Techorama and Code BEAM explicitly).
- **Want the highest-signal architecture CFP in DACH?** → Wait one week, submit to [**iSAQB SAG**](https://www.software-architecture-gathering.com/) when it opens 5 May [[2]](https://www.software-architecture-gathering.com/call-for-papers/). 9 Jun deadline.
- **Want the highest-signal practitioner audience in BE?** → [**Devoxx Belgium**](https://devoxx.be/), CFP opens 1 Jun [[10]](https://devoxx.be/). The architecture track competes for slots; expect serious selection.
- **Just want to attend?** → [DDD Europe](https://2026.dddeurope.com/) (Antwerp, 10-12 Jun) [[7]](https://2026.dddeurope.com/) is the densest design-and-architecture event in BE/FR/NL/DE during the first half of 2026; [ICSA Amsterdam](https://conf.researchr.org/home/icsa-2026) (22-26 Jun) [[3]](https://conf.researchr.org/home/icsa-2026) immediately after if you want the research side; [iSAQB SAG](https://www.software-architecture-gathering.com/) (Berlin, Nov 16-19) [[1]](https://www.software-architecture-gathering.com/) and [W-JAX](https://jax.de/munich/) (Munich, Nov 2-6) [[13]](https://jax.de/munich/) carry the autumn.
