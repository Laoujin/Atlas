> **TL;DR** — Of the .NET / Microsoft-stack conferences physically held in Belgium, France, Netherlands or Germany in 2026, only two big-stage CFPs are still open today: [**Techorama 2026 Netherlands**](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) (Utrecht, Oct 26-28; CFP closes 29 May 2026) [[1]](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) and [**DDC Cologne 2026**](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/) (Nov 23-26; CFP closes 15 Jun 2026, only just opened on Apr 22) [[2]](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/). Paris [**MWCP**](https://modern-workplace.pro/) has a rolling "Appel à Speaker" with no published deadline [[8]](https://modern-workplace.pro/). Everything else is closed ([Techorama BE](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-belgium/), [DWX](https://sessionize.com/dwx-26/), [BASTA Mainz](https://basta.net/mainz-en/), [ESPC Amsterdam](https://espc.tech/conference/espc-2026/), [ECS Cologne](https://collabsummit.eu/), [dataMinds](https://sessionize.com/dataminds-saturday-2026/)), already past ([BASTA Frankfurt](https://basta.net/frankfurt-en/), [Future Tech 2026](https://sessionize.com/future-tech-2026/), Microsoft AI Tour Paris), or not yet announced ([CloudBrew](https://www.cloudbrew.be/), [Future Tech 2027](https://futuretech.nl/), [VisugXL](https://www.visug.be/)).

**Submitter notes**

- [**Techorama NL**](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) covers travel + hotel for accepted speakers and accepts 60-min sessions, 60-min keynotes, and 1-day workshops [[1]](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/).
- [**DDC Cologne**](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/) offers free conference access, catering, accommodation and reasonable travel reimbursement — but no honorarium; format is 60-min sessions, full-day workshops, or DevSessions, with 500–1800-char abstracts [[2]](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/).
- [**Power Summit**](https://sessionize.com/power-summit-14-15-juli-2026/) / [**Azure Summit**](https://sessionize.com/azure-summit-24-25-november-2026/) are German-language online events run by ppedv targeting 1,000–1,500 active participants per event; sessions are 30 min with preference for customer talks [[19]](https://sessionize.com/power-summit-14-15-juli-2026/) [[18]](https://sessionize.com/azure-summit-24-25-november-2026/).
- [**MWCP Paris**](https://modern-workplace.pro/) is the main French Microsoft community event — 50+ sessions on AI, Modern Work and Cybersecurity at the 10th edition [[8]](https://modern-workplace.pro/) [[16]](https://dev.events/EU/FR/microsoft).
- [**VISUG**](https://www.visug.be/) has not yet published a 2026 VisugXL date — the user group is using its 20-year anniversary as a hook for "extra" community events; subscribe to the newsletter to catch the announcement [[20]](https://www.visug.be/).

## Country digest

**Belgium** has the densest .NET community calendar ([Techorama](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-belgium/), [CloudBrew](https://www.cloudbrew.be/), [VISUG](https://www.visug.be/), [dataMinds](https://sessionize.com/dataminds-saturday-2026/)), but the major CFPs all closed in Q4 2025 / Q1 2026. Only VISUG's rolling Sessionize submissions remain truly open right now [[3]](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-belgium/) [[12]](https://www.cloudbrew.be/) [[20]](https://www.visug.be/) [[21]](https://sessionize.com/dataminds-saturday-2026/).

**France** has very thin .NET-specific conference activity — most Microsoft France events ([MWCP](https://modern-workplace.pro/), Microsoft AI Tour, Power Platform French Summit, aMP Day Strasbourg) skew toward Modern Work / Power Platform / AI rather than core .NET development. [**MWCP**](https://modern-workplace.pro/) **is your only practical CFP target right now** [[8]](https://modern-workplace.pro/) [[16]](https://dev.events/EU/FR/microsoft). Note: NewCrafts Paris is on hiatus in 2026 and returns in 2027.

**Netherlands** is the cleanest pick: [Techorama NL](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) has just opened submissions and covers travel + hotel; [ESPC Amsterdam](https://espc.tech/conference/espc-2026/) is large but already closed [[1]](https://sessionize.com/techorama-2026-netherlands/) [[10]](https://espc.tech/conference/espc-2026/).

**Germany** has the deepest .NET conference market in Europe ([DWX](https://sessionize.com/dwx-26/), [BASTA Spring](https://basta.net/frankfurt-en/) + [Herbst](https://basta.net/mainz-en/), [DDC](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/), [ADC](https://adc.ms/26/), [ECS](https://collabsummit.eu/), [M365 Summit](https://sessionize.com/m365-summit-mainz-12-14-oktober-2026/), plus the ppedv online summits) [[15]](https://dev.events/EU/dotnet). Most physical CFPs have already closed for 2026; [**DDC Cologne**](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/) **is the big remaining .NET CFP**, with [M365 Summit Mainz](https://sessionize.com/m365-summit-mainz-12-14-oktober-2026/) still wide open if your topic is M365/Copilot rather than core .NET [[2]](https://sessionize.com/dotnet-developer-conference-26/) [[17]](https://sessionize.com/m365-summit-mainz-12-14-oktober-2026/).
