> **TL;DR (Decision):** If you want to speak at a frontend-leaning conference in BE/FR/NL/DE, five CFPs are still open as of 30 Apr 2026. **React Day Berlin** is the only React-branded one [[1]](https://reactday.berlin/). For broad frontend, **Frontmania (Utrecht)** [[2]](https://sessionize.com/frontmania-2026) and **code.talks (Hamburg)** [[3]](https://codetalks.com/) close first (mid-May / late-May). Both **DevFest Paris** [[4]](https://devfest.gdgparis.fr/) and **DevFest Belgium** [[5]](https://conference-hall.io/devfest-belgium-2026) carry frontend tracks and stay open into late June. The marquee Amsterdam/Paris events (JSNation, React Summit, Vue Amsterdam, React Paris, dotJS) are either past or have closed CFPs for 2026 — submit early for next year.

## Notes on fit

- **Pure-React submitters**: only **React Day Berlin** in 2026 — and you have until August. The other four are general frontend / community tracks where React content competes with Angular/Vue/Svelte/etc.
- **Pure-frontend audience**: **Frontmania** is the only "frontend-only" event with an open CFP; everything else is multi-track.
- **Travel-friendly deadlines**: code.talks (May 15) and Frontmania (May 31) close first — submit those first if you'd like to reuse the same talk at later events.
- **If you missed dotJS / NDC Oslo / Devoxx France**: Devoxx Belgium opens its CFP on June 1, 2026 [[19]](https://devoxx.be/) — a large general dev conference in Antwerp with strong web/frontend tracks historically. Watch that date.

## Methodology

Cross-checked each conference against the official site, the CFP platform (Sessionize / Conference Hall / PaperCall / GitNation), and the cfp.watch aggregator [[7]](https://cfp.watch/). Where the official site lacked a deadline (DevFest Belgium, code.talks), the cfp.watch listing supplied it; flagged with ⚠. Excluded: meetups, hackathons, conferences without a public CFP (e.g. SmashingConf books speakers by invitation). Included only events whose CFP window contains 2026-04-30 in BE/FR/NL/DE.
