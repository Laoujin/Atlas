TL;DR — Five picks for a Europe-based senior dev / architect deciding where to go between May 2026 and Q1 2027:

→ Pure architecture, broadest program: iSAQB Software Architecture Gathering, Berlin, 16–19 Nov 2026[3].

→ Domain modelling depth: DDD Europe, Antwerp, 8–12 Jun 2026[2].

→ Architecture × engineering leadership: GOTO Copenhagen, 28 Sep–2 Oct 2026[6].

→ Best value, broad signal: Craft Conference, Budapest, 4–5 Jun 2026, €499 + VAT[7].

→ Research × industry crossover: ICSA 2026, Amsterdam, 22–26 Jun 2026[4].

QCon London[1], OOP Munich, NDC London and Voxxed Days Amsterdam already happened in early 2026 — see the bottom of this page.

The premier picks

iSAQB Software Architecture Gathering practitioner

16–19 Nov 2026 · Titanic Chaussee Berlin · €799–€1,999

The most architecture-pure event on this list: 7 tracks, 40+ sessions, 50+ speakers, run by iSAQB with heise medien[3]. Workshops on Day 1, conference Days 2–3, deep-dive workshops Day 4. CPSA-aligned, but English-speaking and international.

DDD Europe DDD

8–12 Jun 2026 · Antwerp · workshops Jun 8–10, conference Jun 10–12

The world's leading DDD conference — strategic design, event-driven architecture, socio-technical systems[2]. Aimed squarely at experienced engineers and architects in complex domains. Sister event Data Mesh Live runs Jun 11–12 in the same venue.

GOTO Copenhagen practitioner

28 Sep – 2 Oct 2026 · TAP1 · ~1,200 attendees

"Engineering for Reality" theme: distributed systems, observability, resilience, agentic software, green computing[6]. Strong mix of staff-level engineers, architects, and team leads.

Craft Conference practitioner

4–5 Jun 2026 · Hungarian Railway Museum, Budapest · €499 + VAT

Two days, ~2,000 attendees, headline keynote by Gergely Orosz (The Pragmatic Engineer); Neal Ford and Kenny Baas-Schwegler also confirmed[7]. Best price-to-signal ratio of the practitioner-tier events.

ICSA 2026 research+industry

22–26 Jun 2026 · VU Amsterdam

The IEEE/ACM-aligned working conference. Long tradition of putting researchers and software architects in the same room[4]. 13 tracks including a dedicated "Software Architecture in Practice" track.

ECSA 2026 research+industry

7–11 Sep 2026 · Free University of Bolzano, Italy

20th edition of the European academic flagship[5]. Industry track sits alongside the main research program; new "Registered Reports" track this year. More academic than ICSA, but still attends to practitioner concerns.

Pick by what you want out of it

"I want pure software architecture content, in English"

iSAQB Software Architecture Gathering (Berlin, Nov)[3] is the cleanest match. ICSA (Amsterdam, Jun)[4] if you don't mind academic flavour and like the working-conference format.

"DDD, event storming, modelling complex domains"

DDD Europe Antwerp Jun 8–12[2]. Smaller-scale community alternative: KanDDDinsky in Berlin (community-driven, dates pending)[19].

"Architecture in the broader engineering-leadership context"

GOTO Copenhagen[6] or Craft Conference[7]. Both blend technical depth with team/leadership tracks.

"German-speaking community, CPSA orbit"

iSAQB Software Architecture Forum in Munich (Jun 16–17)[21] is the new community-tier event — 23 sessions, CPSA-aligned[14]. OOP (next edition Feb 2–5, 2027 in Munich, MOC venue) remains the marquee German-speaking architecture event[13].

"Java ecosystem with arch tracks"

Devoxx Belgium (Antwerp, Oct 5–9)[9] is the flagship; JAX London (Oct 5–9, hybrid)[8] if you want London + remote option. Devoxx UK (May 6–7)[10] is a tighter 2-day version.

"Cheap, regional, still substantive"

Voxxed Days Bucharest tagged 50% architecture content at €120–180 a ticket[16] (Apr 28–29 — already past). The Voxxed Days circuit (Zürich, CERN, Ticino, Amsterdam) covers architecture every year at this price point.

QCon — what's the deal

QCon London ran 16–18 March 2026 with 75+ speakers across 15 tracks including Architecture in the Age of AI, Architecting for Resilience, and Debugging Distributed Systems[1][15]. It's the European stop of QCon and remains the practitioner-focused event of choice for senior engineers and architects — InfoQ-curated, very strong for production-system patterns. The 20th-anniversary 2026 edition leaned hard into agentic AI and platform ROI[22]. QCon London 2027 typically runs in mid-March; if architecture is your full-time job, put it on the calendar now.
