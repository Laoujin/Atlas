The lineage tree.

Where every chef on this docket trained

If they aren’t a Flemish Foodie, they’re a Hof van Cleve cook.

Two roots produce nearly the entire Ghent & East-Flanders chef map. Knowing which root tells you what the food will taste like.
Root 01 · Hof van Cleve / Goossens

The technical root.

Peter Goossens’s Kruisem kitchen produced a generation. Then Goossens stepped back 1 Jan 2024 and the room reset to 2★ under Floris Van Der Veken.[9]

Root 02 · The Flemish Foodies

The philosophy root.

Three friend-chefs photographed together in the late 2000s. Same credo — simple, seasonal, local-produce — credited with putting Ghent on the global food map.[31]

The drive-out call sheet.

Within ~90 minutes · mileage from Ghent centre

Worth burning fuel for.

Eight tables on the road. The chef story is the reason to put them on the itinerary — not the rating.
~30km SW

Hof van Cleve

Floris Van Der Veken
Kruisem
★★
The regional benchmark every Ghent chef trained at or competes with. Held 3★ from 2005 to 2023 under Goossens; reset to 2★ under Van Der Veken in the 2024 guide. Still the closest top-tier table.[9][10]
hofvancleve.com michelin
~50km W

Boury

Tim Boury
Roeselare
★★★
One of only two 3★ tables in Belgium (with Zilte, Antwerp). Jumped from 2★ to 3★ in the 2022 guide and held since. A brick villa in West Flanders; classical technique with sauces as the headline.[11][12]
michelin
~55km SE

Eliane

Kobe Desramaults
Brussels
★
First solo restaurant after closing Chambre Séparée end-2020. 22 seats, ~half at the bar counter, ~18-course tasting menu changes daily; 3-month booking window. G&M Remarkable Newcomer of the Year 2026 for Brussels.[19][20][21]
elianerestaurant.be gaultmillau.be
~50km S

Eliane @ Hof ter Bree-Eik

Desramaults · pop-up
Lennik · Pajottenland
Jul–Aug 2026 · pop-up
Summer 2026 only: Eliane temporarily relocates to a vierkantshoeve in the Pajottenland. 40 covers, product-driven tasting menu with local Pajottenland produce. Eliane Brussels closes during the relocation. Book early.[23]
vrt.be
~60km E

Hertog Jan at Botanic

De Mangeleer + Boudens
Antwerp · Botanic Sanctuary
★★
Held 3★ at original Hertog Jan in Zedelgem from 2012 until December 2018; reopened inside Botanic Sanctuary fall 2021. Omakase — "I leave it to the chef" — Asian accents, daring combinations, intimate room.[13][14]
wikipedia botanicantwerp.be
~60km E

Le Pristine

Sergio Herman (Vetrugno, line)
Antwerp
★
Sergio Herman’s "New Italian" project — tribute to Italian flavours through his Zeeland terroir. Open-plan kitchen, Baroque covered patio, all-day café. The casual-luxe end of the Antwerp star map.[16][17]
lepristine.com
~60km E

Privéprivée

Sergio Herman
Antwerp · private room
Booking is the bottleneck
Herman’s private dining room beneath his Antwerp apartment — opened 2024, max 24 guests, exclusive monthly dates. The way to eat Herman’s own cooking again after a decade off the line.[32]
worldofmouth.app
~70km E

Pastorale

Bart De Pooter
Reet
★★ + Green ★
De Pooter has run Pastorale out of his native town’s former presbytery since 1992 — first star 2003, second 2006. G&M Chef of the Year 2012; G&M Entrepreneur of the Year 2023. Marked by balance, simplicity, pureness.[18]
