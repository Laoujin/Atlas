A speakeasy, a hotel-bar, a canal-side kitchen, a rooftop, and an Indian sharing-plate room — the bars in Ghent that take their kitchens as seriously as their bottles.

Editor's
verdict

Drinks-first, then dinner: Jigger's for the cocktails. Drinks and a real kitchen on equal footing: Bar & Kitchen André on the Lys, or Yalo Rooftop for sharing plates with a view. View-and-tapas pre-dinner: The Cobbler. Indian sharing plates with a mixologist duo: Mission Masala.

★ The reference 01JIGGER'S
Patershol · Oudburg 16

Jigger's

Ghent's reference cocktail address.
"Listed by World's 50 Best Discovery. Weekly-changing menu, seasonal & foraged ingredients, regular guest-chef collabs."

If you only book one drinks-first night out in Ghent, this is the consensus pin. Bar bites in lieu of a kitchen — the food is the rotating chef collab, not a fixed menu. Don't come hungry; come thirsty and curious.

The supporting cast.

Four rooms where the bar and the kitchen split the bill 50/50.

02 · Bar & Kitchen André

Patershol · Oudburg 90 (canal-side)

Bar food plus "real dishes" since André expanded the menu — a one-stop apero-into-dinner with a water view.

03 · Yalo Rooftop

Yalo Urban Boutique Hotel · 6th floor

Chef Jasper Maatman: small sharing plates & "classics with a twist". The rooftop bar has a full kitchen and city views — design-hotel vibe without staying over.

Cocktails"Pure" cocktails alongside the kitchen
Use it forsummer rooftop dinner
yalohotel.com rooftop

04 · The Cobbler

1898 The Post · Graslei 16

Refined bites and fine tapas in a neo-Gothic former post office. Signature drinks (Dirty Secrets — Szechuan pepper, aged soy) and a view over the spires.

FormatBites, not sit-down
Use it forpre-dinner cocktail with the city's best view
1898thepost.com visit.gent.be

05 · Mission Masala

Ghent (also Brussels & Antwerp)

Indian-tapas sharing concept with an in-house mixologist duo. Cocktails are billed as an equal half of the concept — a group dinner where everyone wants something different and a proper drink.
