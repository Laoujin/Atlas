TL;DR. Drinks-first, then dinner: Jigger's for the cocktails (seasonal, foraged, weekly menu, regular guest-chef collabs[1]). Drinks and a real kitchen on equal footing: Bar and Kitchen André on the Lys (natural wines + a proper menu beyond bar food)[2] or Yalo Rooftop for sharing plates with a view[3]. View-and-tapas pre-dinner: The Cobbler at 1898 The Post[4]. Indian sharing plates with a mixologist duo: Mission Masala[5].

The picks

Jigger's[1] Oudburg 16, Patershol Bar bites; regular guest-chef collaborations rather than a fixed menu[1] Weekly-changing menu, seasonal & foraged ingredients, listed by 50 Best Discovery[1] Drinks-first night out — the city's reference cocktail address
Bar and Kitchen André[2] Oudburg 90, Patershol (canal-side, looking onto the Lys) Bar food plus "real dishes" since André expanded the menu → full-dinner viable[2] Cocktails plus an extensive natural-wine list[2] One-stop apero-into-dinner with a water view
Yalo Restaurant & Rooftop[3] Yalo Urban Boutique Hotel, central Chef Jasper Maatman: small sharing plates & "classics with a twist", seasonal regional sourcing[3] "Pure cocktails" alongside the kitchen; 6th-floor rooftop bar with full kitchen and city views[6] Summer rooftop dinner; design-hotel vibe without staying over
The Cobbler[4] 1898 The Post, Graslei 16 Refined bites / fine tapas — not a sit-down dinner room[4] Signature drinks (e.g. Dirty Secrets w/ Szechuan pepper & aged soy[7]); neo-Gothic post-office building, view over the spires[4] Pre-dinner cocktail with the best view in town (5pm onwards[4])
Mission Masala[5] Ghent (also Brussels & Antwerp) Indian-tapas sharing concept with a modern twist; evenings only → reserve[5] In-house mixologist duo; cocktails are a billed-equal half of the concept[5] Group dinner where everyone wants something different and a proper drink

Honourable mentions (cocktails-only, no real food)

Visit Gent's own short-list also flags The Drifter (tiki, rum-led), Limonada, Polé Polé (Latin/African vibe) and Eltòn as aperitif spots[7] — great for a first or last drink, but plan dinner elsewhere.

How to read this list

"Cocktail-led" here means the drinks programme is at least as serious as the kitchen. Jigger's anchors the cocktail end (a 50 Best Discovery listing[1]); André and Yalo anchor the dinner end (full kitchens, chef-led[2][3]); The Cobbler and Mission Masala sit in between (tapas / sharing-plate format alongside an ambitious bar[4][5]). Patershol clusters two of the five (Jigger's, André), so a single neighbourhood crawl is realistic.
