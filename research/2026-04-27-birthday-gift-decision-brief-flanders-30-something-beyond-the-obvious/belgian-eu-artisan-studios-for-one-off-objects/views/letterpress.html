The fastest paths
from Belgium

Stamped & certified
by the makers themselves

For a real one-off birthday gift, the easiest paths from Belgium are: a piece of jewellery from Saskia Shutt (Brussels, ~6 weeks, Fairmined materials)[8]; a made-to-order leather object from Atelier Decoster (any leather, optional 22k-gold initials)[6]; or a ceramic from La Gadoue at the Au Charbon collective in Anderlecht.[4][5]

For something larger — wood furniture, glass art, tapestry — go to Verduyn or Shedded for wood[10][11], Aristide Najean's Murano furnace for glass[15], or Galerij Theaxus for hand-woven tapestry (4-5 months for the largest pieces)[14]. Plan around lead time first — most studios need 4-12 weeks minimum.

Chapter III  ·  House Procedure

How to commission a piece.

Five steps shared across nearly every studio above. Skipping step four — the deposit conversation — is the single most common cause of timeline drift.

01

Pick discipline first

Decide whether the gift is jewellery, leather, ceramic, glass, wood, or textile before browsing studios.

Lead times are wildly different. Jewellery is ~6 weeks[8]; large tapestry is 4–5 months[14].

02

Reach out 8–16 wk before

Most studios reply within a week with a brief form, then schedule a consult.

Saskia Shutt's six weeks is post-design — design itself adds time.[8] Verduyn's five-step process includes a 3D-render round before fabrication.[10]

03

Bring material, not a brief

Inherited gold, a fabric scrap, a photo of the recipient's space, a colour palette.

Saskia Shutt explicitly remodels family heirlooms[8]; La Gadoue's textile side works with discarded fabric scraps[22].

04

Expect a deposit

Typical 30–50% upfront for materials and slot reservation. Ask explicitly.

Public sites rarely list pricing; quote-on-request is the norm at Decoster[6], Louie[7], Verduyn[10], Shedded[11], Wave Murano[16].

05

Write down "one-off"

Confirm in writing: is the piece truly unique, or a small-edition variant?

Some studios (Bautier, Muller Van Severen) are collection- or edition-led despite handmade production.[17] Get the distinction in the contract.
