How to pick in 60 seconds.

  • < 7 days lead time → Songfinch rush ($274), star map, class booking, or a balloon-voucher combo.
  • Relationship < 1 year → avoid bespoke perfume and $1k+ commissions (overshoots). Default: custom song, shared class, or star map.
  • "I don't want more stuff" → experience tier only — balloon, class, museum after-hours. Skip personalised objects.
  • Budget > $500 & ≥ 2 mo → bespoke perfume or premium portrait. The two she literally cannot buy herself.
  • When in doubt → custom song + shared class. ~$300, hits both halves of "something that acknowledges her + something you do together."
